❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed?
A valid driving license (original) and one government ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN) are required at the time of pickup.
What is the minimum age?
You must be at least 21 years old with a valid driving license to rent a self-drive car.
What is the fuel policy?
The car is provided with a certain fuel level. You need to return it with the same fuel level. Fuel cost is borne by the customer.
Is security deposit required?
Yes, a refundable security deposit is collected at the time of pickup and returned upon safe return of the vehicle.
What is the km limit?
Standard packages include a daily km limit. Extra km charges apply beyond the limit. Contact us for unlimited km packages.
Can I extend my booking?
Yes! Just message us on WhatsApp before your return time and we'll extend your booking based on availability.
